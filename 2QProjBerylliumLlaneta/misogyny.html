MISOGYNY

Impact on Women

Expectations and Standards

Misogynistic ideals see women as inferior compared to men and
this creates the stigma of women having less capabilities and they
are reduced to being 'housewives,' who should know how to cook, clean
and work around the house. It expects that women stay in the house and
take care of children while men change the world. This means that women
basically have a single purpose, to help men, which makes women seem more
like objects for men. Because of the low expectations, society imagine men when
thinking about 'successful' jobs.

The oppression against women as being 'less capable' holds a lot
of women back from things they can do and just the fact that being a woman
is an inferior role but was never their choice, makes them feel helpless, and unworthy.
This hinders humanity in advancing different fields to their max potential. Misogynistic
mentality can lead to rape and slut shaming, which focuses more on blaming women for what
they want to wear rather than punishing the behavior of men who are doing the wrong.
This oppression could also severely harm women's mental health.
